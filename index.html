GBM Price Path Simulator

A live implementation of Geometric Brownian Motion — the same stochastic process used in my Trading Bot project. Each path follows dS = μS dt + σS dW where dW is a Wiener process, discretised as S(t+dt) = S(t) · exp((μ − σ²/2)dt + σ√dt · Z), Z ~ N(0,1). The green line is the mean path; the shaded band is the ±1σ envelope.
